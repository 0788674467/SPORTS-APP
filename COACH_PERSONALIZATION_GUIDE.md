# Coach Personalization Guide - Quick Reference

## 🎯 What You Can Do

As a coach, you can now personalize your account and team branding directly from your dashboard.

---

## 📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1️⃣ Access Settings
- Click the **Settings** icon (⚙️) in the left sidebar
- Or click the **Settings** option in the navigation menu
- Scroll down to see all personalization options

---

### 2️⃣ Team Branding

#### Upload Team Badge/Logo
1. Scroll to **Team Branding** section
2. Click the badge area (shows "TAP TO UPLOAD")
3. Select an image from your gallery
4. Preview appears before saving
5. Click **Save Team Branding** button
6. ✅ Badge now displays in your dashboard overview

#### Change Team Name
1. In **Team Branding** section, find the "Team Name" field
2. Clear the current name and type your new team name
3. Click **Save Team Branding** button
4. ✅ Team name updates everywhere in the app

---

### 3️⃣ Coach Profile

#### Update Your Photo
1. Scroll to **Coach Profile** section
2. Click your profile photo (avatar)
3. Choose:
   - **Take a Photo** - Use your camera
   - **Choose from Gallery** - Select from your device
4. Photo uploads automatically
5. ✅ Your new photo appears in the sidebar and header

#### Edit Your Name
1. In **Coach Profile** section, find "Full Name" field
2. Update your name
3. Click **Save Profile** button
4. ✅ Name updates in your profile and sidebar

#### Update Phone Number
1. In **Coach Profile** section, find "Phone Number" field
2. Enter your phone number
3. Click **Save Profile** button
4. ✅ Phone number is saved

---

### 4️⃣ Change Password

1. Scroll to **Change Password** section
2. Enter your new password in "New Password" field
3. Re-enter it in "Confirm New Password" field
4. Click the eye icon to show/hide passwords
5. Password must be at least 6 characters
6. Click **Update Password** button
7. ✅ Password changed successfully

---

### 5️⃣ Theme Preference

1. Scroll to **Appearance** section
2. Toggle the switch to change background:
   - **ON** = Light background
   - **OFF** = White background
3. ✅ Dashboard theme updates immediately

---

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Photo Tips
- **Best Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Recommended Size**: 400x400 pixels or larger
- **File Size**: Keep under 5MB for faster upload
- **Profile Photo**: Use a clear headshot
- **Team Badge**: Use a square image for best results

### Team Name Tips
- Keep it concise (under 50 characters)
- Use your university/college name
- Avoid special characters
- Examples: "Tech University FC", "State College United"

### Password Tips
- Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid common words or personal information
- Don't reuse old passwords
- Write it down somewhere safe

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Data Synchronization
- All changes sync automatically to the database
- Updates appear across all pages within seconds
- Your profile updates are visible to admins and spectators

### Permissions
- You can only edit your own profile
- You can only edit your team's branding
- Admins can view but not modify your personal settings

### Storage
- Photos are stored securely in the cloud
- Badges are publicly visible to spectators
- Profile photos are visible to admins and other coaches

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### Photo Won't Upload
- Check file size (should be under 5MB)
- Ensure you have internet connection
- Try a different image format (JPG or PNG)
- Refresh the page and try again

### Changes Not Saving
- Check that all required fields are filled
- Ensure you clicked the Save button
- Look for error messages in red
- Try refreshing the page

### Password Change Failed
- Ensure passwords match
- Password must be at least 6 characters
- Try a different password
- Contact admin if issue persists

### Photo Not Showing
- Wait a few seconds for upload to complete
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try uploading a different image
